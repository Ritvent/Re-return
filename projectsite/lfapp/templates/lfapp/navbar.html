How HanApp Works

1

Report an Item

Lost something? Post a "Lost Item" report. Found something? Post a "Found Item" report.

2

Connect

Use the in-app messaging to communicate with other users and notify their email about the item.

3

Return or Claim

Meet up to resolve the item. Once done, mark the item as "Claimed" or "Returned" to close the case.
